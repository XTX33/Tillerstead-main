How We Work

Documented Craftsmanship from Walk-through to Closeout

  1. Walk-through & Assessment

    Start with a free on-site consultation to review your goals, budget, and property conditions. I identify any underlying issues so your project is built on solid information from day one.

  2. Written Scope & Approval

    Receive a detailed written scope covering materials, timelines, waterproofing assemblies, and clear change-order terms. You always know what’s included, when it’s coming, and why.
