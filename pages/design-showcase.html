Our Technical Approach

Design Philosophy

Every Tillerstead project is built on TCNA guidelines, NJ HIC compliance, and a commitment to assemblies that outlast trends and withstand real-world use.

Precision Layout

We use laser-guided layout and digital planning to ensure every tile aligns with millimeter accuracy—no lippage, no guesswork, no shortcuts.

Waterproofing Integrity

All assemblies exceed TCNA and manufacturer specs for waterproofing. We document every step, from membrane installation to flood testing, protecting your investment and meeting NJ code.

Signature Patterns

Our Sacred Tile pattern and custom layouts are engineered for both visual impact and structural integrity, balancing aesthetics with maintenance and longevity.

Technical Excellence

Craftsmanship Details

Every layer matters. Our assemblies—from substrate to grout—are built to TCNA standards and NJ HIC requirements, ensuring lasting performance and legal compliance.

Substrate Preparation

We verify substrates for flatness, deflection, and moisture content, using ASTM-compliant methods before any tile is set.

Waterproof Membrane Systems

Tillerstead installs ANSI A118.10-compliant membranes, with special attention to corners, penetrations, and transitions—documented for your records.

Layout & Pattern Planning

We optimize layouts to minimize waste, avoid awkward cuts, and ensure visual balance—always referencing TCNA Handbook details for your assembly type.

Grout & Seal Protection

Only premium, manufacturer-approved grouts and sealers are used, matched to your tile and use case for maximum stain and moisture resistance.
