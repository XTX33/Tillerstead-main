TCNA-Compliant Financing for Tile & Bath Work

Proper prep, waterproofing, and layout are non-negotiable for lasting results. If your budget is tight, we’ll phase the project or connect you with reputable financing—never at the expense of code compliance or best practices.

Tillerstead never marks up rates or uses “teaser” pricing. Every estimate is written, detailed, and NJ HIC-compliant. You decide: pay in full, phase the work, or apply for third-party financing.

How Financing Works

  1. Scope the project: We inspect your space, discuss goals, and outline phases as needed—always referencing TCNA and manufacturer specs.
  2. Transparent pricing: You receive a written, line-item estimate covering labor, materials, and all options—no hidden fees.
  3. Choose your payment method: Pay in full, phase the work, or apply for financing through Acorn Finance’s secure platform.

Financing is always optional. We never compromise on waterproofing, substrate prep, or code compliance to “fit” a rushed budget.
