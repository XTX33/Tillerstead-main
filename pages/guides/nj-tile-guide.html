1 TCNA & ANSI Standards

What is TCNA?

Tile Council of North America provides installation method standards.

  • TCNA Handbook: Installation methods
  • Method EJ171: Standard shower installation
  • Method B421: Waterproof membranes

Key ANSI Standards

  • A108.10: Tile installation
  • A118.10: Waterproofing membranes
  • A118.15: Large format adhesive

2 Vetting NJ Contractors

3 Waterproofing Standards

ANSI A118.10: All showers require compliant waterproofing.

Popular Systems

  • Schluter KERDI: Sheet membrane, professional grade
  • RedGard: Liquid, DIY-friendly
  • Laticrete Hydroban: Crack suppression
  • Wedi Board: Pre-waterproofed substrate
Must-Haves: Continuous membrane, corner sealing, extends 6"+ above shower head, flood tested before tile.

4 Material Selection

Porcelain Tile

Pros: Durable, low absorption
Best for: Floors, wet areas

Ceramic Tile

Pros: Affordable, easy to work
Best for: Walls, backsplashes

Large Format (LFT)

Requires ANSI A118.15 adhesive, extremely flat substrate, back-buttering, experienced installer.

6 NJ Legal Rights

  • Written Contract: Required for jobs over $500
  • Deposit Limits: Max 1/3 or $1,000
  • 3-Day Cancel: Right to cancel contracts signed at home
  • Warranty: Must be provided in writing
If Problems: Document everything, communicate in writing, file complaint with NJ Consumer Affairs: 1-800-242-5846
