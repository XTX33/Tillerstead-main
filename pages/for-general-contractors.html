Estimates, Scheduling & Change Orders

Tillerstead’s process for general contractors is transparent, documented, and built for accountability:

  • Estimates: Submit plans or takeoffs. Proposals include written scope, substrate and waterproofing requirements, and material allowances—referencing TCNA and ANSI standards.
  • Scheduling: Target dates confirmed in writing with a readiness checklist. Automated reminders sent one week and one day before mobilization.
  • Change Orders: All scope changes documented same-day, with clear pricing and written approval—ensuring billing matches actual work performed.
  • Daily Communication: Brief text or email updates with progress, inspection status, material needs, and next-trade handoff notes.
  • Punchlists: Completion includes photo documentation, cleanup, and a signed closeout report for straightforward project turnover.
