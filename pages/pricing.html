  • Substrate Condition: Solid, flat substrate vs. repair/replacement needed
  • Layout Complexity: Standard grid vs. custom herringbone/patterns
  • Access & Logistics: Ground floor vs. upper floors, parking, material delivery
  • Project Scope: Tile-only vs. full bathroom gut renovation
  • Timeline: Standard 3-4 week lead vs. rush scheduling
